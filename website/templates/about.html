About AdoPet

AdoPet is a platform dedicated to connecting people who want to adopt pets that need a new home. Our goal is to make the adoption process easier, making it simple, safe and transparent for everyone involved.

Here you can search for available pets, filter by name, species, age and size, as well as view details and photos of the animals. If you have a pet to donate, you can also easily register it on our platform.

Join us in this mission to provide a better life for many animals!
